Who We Are

Built for Cricketers Who Refuse
to Stand Still

Model Town Whites Cage Cricket Club sits in the heart of Model Town, Lahore — a dedicated, purpose-built cage cricket arena engineered for serious practice. Whether you're a weekend player chasing rhythm or a competitive batter grinding reps against the machine, our caged pitches, floodlit nets, and premium turf give you a controlled, high-repetition environment to train exactly the way the game demands.

Cage-Controlled Precision

Fully enclosed cricket cages mean every ball stays in play — more reps, zero wasted time chasing shots.

Day & Night Access

Floodlit facilities keep the game alive well after sunset, seven days a week.

Community Driven

A home ground for Model Town's cricket community — memberships, squads, and regular players welcome.

Prime Model Town Location

Located on Model Town Circular Road, Block E — easy to reach, easy to make a habit of.
